About HHIL

Hindware Home Innovation Limited is a leader in Building Products and one of the fastest growing players in the Indian Consumer Appliances segment. The company is focused on servicing consumers and is involved in manufacturing, branding, marketing, sales & distribution, and service of various product categories.

The Consumer Appliances business consists of a selection of household appliances such as kitchen appliances: chimneys, built-in hobs, dishwashers, cooktops, built-in ovens, cooking range, water purifiers and sinks. The business also houses air coolers and ceiling fans. Hindware Home Innovation Limited* also has a strategic marketing tie-up with leading Italian company, Formenti & Giovenzana in the furniture and kitchen fittings segment. Hintastica Private Limited is a joint venture between Hindware Home Innovation Limited and Groupe Atlantic (France) housing the manufacturing, marketing, and distribution of the water heaters and room heaters business.

Hindware Home Innovation Limited* through its wholly-owned subsidiary (Hindware Limited) (has a versatile range of best-in-class sanitaryware, faucets, and tiles products with brands catering to a wide pricing spectrum of customers. Hindware Limited also houses the plastic pipes& fittings and overhead water storage tanks business under the brand ‘Truflo by Hindware,’ one of the fastest growing brands in the country in this segment.

About Hintastica Private Limited :

Hintastica Private Limited is a joint ventured company with Groupe Atlantic, France’s leading brand that has a dominant presence in manufacturing, developing, and distributing eco-friendly heating products and hot water solutions.

Hinastica Private Limited is the wholly owned subsidiary company of Somany Home Innovation Limited, one of India’s leading premium consumer appliances and building products company, popularly known as ‘Hindware Appliances’

Formed in March 2021, as Hintastica Pvt. Ltd. the 50:50 Joint Venture aims at creating strong customer base by offering innovative products of international standards to Indian consumers as well as to neighboring nations in SAARC region.

The merger aims to leverage the proven expertise of Pioneer companies’ i.e Technology, Manufacturing and Assembly of Groupe Atlantic and Marketing, Distribution and Reach of Hindware Appliances.
