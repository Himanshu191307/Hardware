AGI Greenpac Limited is India’s leading Packaging Products Company that manufactures and markets various packaging products, including glass containers and Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) bottles, products & security caps, and closures.

About AGI Greenpac

In 1981, AGI Greenpac (Formerly HSIL) entered the Packaging Products business by acquiring ‘The Associated Glass Industries Limited’ (AGI), a manufacturer of container glass. AGI Greenpac also added PET bottles into the portfolio by acquiring Garden Polymers Private Limited (GPPL) in 2011.

Recognising a market for counterfeit-resistant caps and closures in developing economies like India, AGI Greenpac ventured into this business by setting up a state-of-the-art facility in Telangana, with the first phase of commercial production commencing in the last quarter of FY 2017-18.

The company is centered on the manufacturing & marketing of various packaging products like glass containers & polyethylene terephthalate (PET) products and also safe & secure caps and closures. With excellence in manufacturing glass products with an impressive scale of 40+ years of experience as India's leading glass container manufacturer, the company aims to provide customers with the most sustainable lifestyle with their unique glass & closure products.

AGI Greenpac successfully manufactured India's first anti-bacterial glass bottle & initiated the manufacturing of Eco-friendly wellness glass containers to save mother earth. The company specializes in products in the following segments:

  • PET bottles
  • Glass bottles for lifestyle
  • Go green security caps with IGBC platinum rating
  • Specialty liquor
  • Beauty
  • Food & Distribution
  • Pharmaceuticals

Leveling up & going green, we manufacture an exclusive product range. The company follows advanced automation technology in every step of the process & adopted the most advanced quality management processing & recognized for our expertise in fulfilling the global satisfactory glass requirements. The company aims to be an agile, profitable & sustainable business while enhancing the stakeholder's value.

A constant focus on market expansion by creating a unique & forward-looking product portfolio with an increased segment of value-added products to improvise & offer conventional and new-age packaging solutions to better serve the client's needs. The company also focuses on employee welfare which enables the employee to live a more prosperous and good work environment and also initiated welfare programs for communities with a long-standing focus on sustainability & stewardship.
