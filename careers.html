OUR WORK ETHOS

At Somany Impresa Group, we believe that our people are our greatest asset and our true strength lies in their potential. We strive to continuously develop and transform our talent by providing them growth opportunities and a high degree of operational freedom. Our aim is to become one of the most attractive employer in our industry. This is one of the key pillars of the corporate strategy for the businesses and brands.

Why Somany Impresa

OPPORTUNITIES

We offer a wide array of opportunities to both young people who are starting their careers and more experienced professionals.

We are a constantly growing company open to change, and we seek and appreciate the diverse perspectives. The growth of the company is parallel to the growth of our people. We focus on encouraging our employees to realize their potential and we work to support the development of talent within the company.

REWARDS AND RECOGNITIONS

We recognize the will to create a difference and, improve each day to complete the never ending journey to reach the acme.

TALENT DEVELOPMENT

Across our businesses, we have tailor-made training programs to help our employees further develop their skill set. And with diverse opportunities, individual developmental programs, and mentoring and coaching, we create leaders.

WORK-LIFE BALANCE

Our people are more than their job titles. They are not just what they do as part of their job responsibility but also the difference they create individually in the society. We focus on ensuring our people can stretch their wings in the sky they want to fly in.
