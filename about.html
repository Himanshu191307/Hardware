About Somany Impresa

India’s leading conglomerate, Somany Impresa Group is home to one of India’s most iconic brands – Hindware. Headquarters out of Gurgaon, Haryana, the Group has three leading companies under its ambit - Hindware Limited, Hindware Home Innovation Limited & AGI Greenpac. It has a presence across Building Products, Consumer Appliances, Packaging Products, and Retail businesses catering to the modern needs of the ever changing Indian landscape.

Hindware Home Innovation Limited and AGI Greenpac are listed both on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) & the National Stock Exchange (NSE). Somany Impresa Group aims to add unparalleled value to the lives of customers and stakeholders through constant innovation in products and manufacturing.

We successfully balance innovation and design to offer thoughtfully crafted products, under well-established brands. Technology is indispensable to all of our businesses and is the key component to accelerating and generating synergy among them. Our vast array of products across segments, price points, marketing muscle, distribution network, and established relationships with existing dealers and customers enable us to grow sustainably. Further, a strategically robust supply chain and outsourced manufacturing help improve productivity and flexibility in our operations. We are increasingly leveraging our businesses equity and scale, while at the same time benefiting from deep consumer understanding to leverage opportunities.

Our Businesses

A constant drive for innovation helps us thrive with high levels of industry recognition. This pursuit of excellence has helped us weather the storm of today’s highly dynamic and evolving world while staying relevant to consumer needs. Our diverse spectrum of products ranges across bathware, home appliances, packaging, and retail.

Our company Hindware Home Innovation Limited's consumer products portfolio spans various areas of home appliances – from kitchen appliances to water heaters and purification products amongst others. Our commitment to innovation is, this will be seen in the 33 patents we've filled for since the inception of the Business.

Under our company Hindware Limited, we have 3 brands span the spectrum of luxury, premium, and mass categories in the bathroom segment. We also offer plastic pipes and fittings segment under the brand TRUFLO and have a robust presence in the super-premium tiles segment with the brand Hindware Italian Tiles.

With about 60 million people and more connecting each minute, Hindware has emerged as one of the most reliable and vibrant brands in the country.

Our company, AGI Greenpac, manufactures and markets various packaging products, including glass containers, Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) bottles, products & security caps, and closures.

Manufacturing Plants

Spread across the country, our state-of-the-art manufacturing plants are committed to bringing to life product designs that solve problems in an efficient and sustainable manner. Across 13 plants, we employ top-of-the-line technology, &D and design facilities to manufacture products to cater to the ask of customers.

For us, sustainability is a prerequisite for being successful in the future and has so been included as a key aspect of the company’s strategy. We are committed to our sustainability goals by setting targets to become water and carbon efficient as we scale up our businesses.

We have two sanitaryware plants at Bahadurgarh in Haryana and Bibinagar in Telangana, one faucet plant in Kaharani in Rajasthan, and two plastic pipes and fittings plants at Sangareddy in Telangana. We have three glass containers plants at Sanathnagar and Bhongir in Telangana, one specialty glass container plant, three PET bottles and products plants at Selaqui in Uttarakhand, Dharwad in Karnataka, and Sangareddy in Telangana, and one security caps and closures facility at Sangareddy in Telangana.
